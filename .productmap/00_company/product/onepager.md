# Product Onepager

## Problem:
Traders in prediction markets often miss arbitrage opportunities due to the fast-moving nature of odds and fragmented information across platforms. This leads to lost profits and inefficient trading.

## Who feels this pain:
Active prediction market traders and arbitrage seekers who monitor multiple markets and need timely alerts to act quickly.

## Severity and urgency:
Missed arbitrage chances can mean significant financial loss or reduced returns. The window to act is often very short, making real-time alerts critical.

## Current workarounds:
Traders manually track odds across platforms or use generic market monitoring tools that lack prediction market focus and real-time arbitrage detection.

## Why this bot is better:
Our Telegram bot delivers instant, targeted arbitrage alerts directly to users’ phones, simplifying monitoring and enabling faster action. It’s lightweight, easy to use, and tailored specifically for prediction markets, unlike generic tools.
